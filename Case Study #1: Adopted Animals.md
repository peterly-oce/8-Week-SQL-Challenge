## Business Task
The company wants to measure themtrics and see some metrics around: average time cats spend in shelter, return/adoption failure rates, satisfaction feedback from adopters. 

## Key goals
The main or key goals of the ERD will be: 
1. We need to track each cat from intake to adoption (possible return)
2. Track adoption events (who adopted, when and what was the outcome)
3. Store feedback from adopters post-adoption
4. Enable calculation of: Avg shelter time, Return Rate, Satisfaction metrics.
